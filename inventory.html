How availability is calculated

Availability is date-based and prevents overbooking.

FormulaStock − Booked − Maintenance = Available
Maintenance includes damaged items, holds, or cleaning time between events.

Rental notes

  • Quantities are per event date and can differ by delivery/pickup windows.
  • Some items require setup time, power access, or venue approvals.
  • For large events, reserve early to lock inventory and delivery slots.
